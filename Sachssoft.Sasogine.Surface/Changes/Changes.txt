*** INFORMATION ***

tcs UIGame is based on Myra and was further developed by Tobias Sachs

This UI framework goes its own way. It continues to be developed and remains free for the others.

However, it was only intended for Monogame. Support for FNA and KNI in next versions is planned.
The rest like Stride and others are no longer supported due to technical reasons. Please understand this.
